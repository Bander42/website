home Strung Up
This was a site-specific installation that existed within a white-void room in the Sharp biulding of SAIC. Consisting of a collection of hoodies and sweatshirts that I had aquired over the years, poloroids, a web-like lattice of string, along with lighting and other objects of comfort, this piece existed as a living embodyment of how i was thinking about my physical and material relationship to comfort, security, home, and memory...
